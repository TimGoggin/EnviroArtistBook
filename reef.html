The Great Barrier Reef

Why is The Great Barrier Reef Important?

The Great Barrier Reef is composed of 3,000 individual reef systems, 760 fringe reefs, 600 tropical islands and about 300 coral cays. It contains more than 6,000 species - many of which are endemic.

The Reef is Dying

50.7% of the reef's coral cover has vanished.

Why is the Reef Dying?

  • Global Warming causes coral to bleach and die
  • Nitrogen Pollution increases algae
    • Algae block seagrasses from sunlight
    • Algae invite crown-of-thorns starfish

How Long Does The Great Barrier Reef Have Left?

Some estimates suggest that the Reef may be gone as early as 2050 if nothing is done.
